Chromatica Choral - Spring 2024 Concert

These are the marketing materials for Chromatica Chorale’s spring 2024 concert. This is a choir based in Danville, CA for whom I volunteer to do their graphic design work. This concert was focused around a commissioned piece of music involving a well known composer, Christopher Tin, and lyricist, Charles Anthony Silvestri, so they wanted to do an early save the date postcard in addition to their normal posters with matching postcard. We made a 24 page printed concert booklet that was distributed during the shows. In addition to formatting the entire booklet and adding some creative elements, such as a decorative stripe along the edges of content pages (to distinguish them from ad pages). I also helped to create some of the ads or resize them from existing business cards and submitted ads.
These are the marketing materials for Chromatica Chorale’s spring 2024 concert. This is a choir based in Danville, CA for whom I volunteer to do their graphic design work. This concert was focused around a commissioned piece of music involving a well known composer, Christopher Tin, and lyricist, Charles Anthony Silvestri, so they wanted to do an early save the date postcard in addition to their normal posters with matching postcard. We made a 24 page printed concert booklet that was distributed during the shows. In addition to formatting the entire booklet and adding some creative elements, such as a decorative stripe along the edges of content pages (to distinguish them from ad pages). I also helped to create some of the ads or resize them from existing business cards and submitted ads.

The marketing campaign was extremely successful. They had a much larger audience than their previous concerts. They printed 400 programs, more than they usually print, but had over 550 attendees.


Chromatica Choral - Fall 2023 Concert

These are the marketing materials for the fall 2023 concert for Chromatica Chorale based in Danville, CA. I am volunteering as the graphic designer for their fall and spring concerts this season. I designed this poster using their pre-existing logo, but chose and edited the image from a video clip I took many years ago and chose the layout and fonts used. I’ve been working with members of their board to present my ideas and get feedback on what they want; we went through many image and design ideas before we finalized this one. After finalizing the image and layout for the poster, I adapted it to fit on a larger poster, postcard, and ticket. I also worked on the design and layout for the printed brochure program handed out at the concerts, as well as a digital booklet program that concert goers could access on their phones.
