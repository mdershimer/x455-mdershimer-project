Chromatica Choral - Spring 2024 Concert

These are the marketing materials for Chromatica Chorale’s spring 2024 concert. This is a choir based in Danville, CA for whom I volunteer to do their graphic design work. This concert was focused around a commissioned piece of music involving a well known composer, Christopher Tin, and lyricist, Charles Anthony Silvestri, so they wanted to do an early save the date postcard in addition to their normal posters with matching postcard. We made a 24 page printed concert booklet that was distributed during the shows. In addition to formatting the entire booklet and adding some creative elements, such as a decorative stripe along the edges of content pages (to distinguish them from ad pages). I also helped to create some of the ads or resize them from existing business cards and submitted ads. The marketing campaign was extremely successful. They had a much larger audience than their previous concerts. They printed 400 programs, more than they usually print, but had over 550 attendees.

I had proposed a number of design ideas for the main theme of the poster, finalists among those included plant life-specifically the process of ferns unfurling, and a rose window styled image with decorative elements showing the transformational life cycle of a butterfly, around a petaled center flower in the different colors of the choir’s logo. The rose window idea was deemed a little gross in having to look at the earlier life cycles with all the larvae and bugs so I proposed a photograph showing a human figure walking out of a dark forested area into a well lit open area, with his long shadow following along behind him. Since the photograph was black and white, this was deemed too dark of a poster overall. A board member suggested using a photograph of a nebula, the transformation of a star, and after hunting through images we ended up on this one where the colors had been enhanced to be bright and bold.


Chromatica Choral - Fall 2023 Concert

These are the marketing materials for Chromatica’s fall 2023 concert. I designed this poster using their pre-existing logo, but chose and edited the image from a video clip I took many years ago and chose the layout and fonts used. The concert was about remembering, both loved ones who have passed and more generally songs composed with the intention of calling back memories. The choir wanted to evoke this sense of recalling the past and loved ones, but did not want the imagery to be sad in itself. I tried a few different ideas more directly evoking memories and the past, but we decided on something more symbolic, with light being blocked but also coming through, kind of like how we interact with our memories. The choir directly also specifically liked the empty bench in this chosen scene, because it was similar to the league he is a part of and how when one of their members passes away they leave their chair empty in their gatherings. If I remember correctly, the image was taken from a graveyard, although that is not evident in this shot. The image was chosen more for how it captures the light. After finalizing the image and layout for the poster, I adapted it to fit on a larger poster, postcard, and ticket. I also worked on the design and layout for the printed three-fold brochure program handed out at the concerts, as well as a digital booklet program that concert goers could access on their phones. The digital booklet included more details about the choir, lyrics for each individual song, images relating to the songs, and some fun tidbits about the music and sometimes how it interacted with other songs on the program or the other concerts the choir has performed.
